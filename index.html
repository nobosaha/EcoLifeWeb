About This Project

EcoLife Adventure helps people learn how to live sustainably by understanding how their daily choices influence the planet. The website combines awareness, real-time calculation tools, and improvement strategies.

Tips for a Greener Life

  • Use public transport or walk for short trips.
  • Reduce food waste and choose plant-based meals more often.
  • Turn off unused lights and appliances.
  • Avoid buying unnecessary items.

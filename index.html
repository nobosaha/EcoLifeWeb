About EcoLife Adventure

This is a lightweight, free web app that encourages responsible daily habits through a short interactive quiz and small challenges. The platform focuses on behaviour change so individuals can reduce waste, save energy, and contribute to climate action.

Our Focused SDGs

12

SDG 12 — Responsible Consumption & Production

How we help: the quiz and micro-challenges encourage less single-use plastics, careful purchasing, food waste reduction, and conscious reuse — all practical behaviours that reduce waste and resource use.

Concrete features: quiz questions, daily challenges, improvement tips, and the pledge board to reinforce behaviour.

13

SDG 13 — Climate Action

How we help: we teach small, climate-positive actions (walk instead of short drive, reduce electricity use, plant-based meals). When many people adopt these, emissions fall.

Concrete features: carbon-aware tips in results, suggested transport/energy behaviours, and repeatable micro-challenges.
